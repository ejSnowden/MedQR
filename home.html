Our philosophy

The end goal of MedQR is to create a functioning medical ID card with a scannable QR code that contains the patient’s medical information in case of an emergency.

The purpose of a MedQR ID card is to provide a faster way to register medical information at clinics/hospitals; eliminating the need to fill out a number of medical forms at the same time.

Insurance information is quickly and effectively transferred to medical institutions in order to facilitate the process of claims.
